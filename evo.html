About the Project

In the spring of 2018, me and 4 other students deisgned and develeoped Evo for our IDEA 350 class. Evo is a top-down action adventure game that teaches the basics of evolution. It uses open-world level design and Metroidvania-style progression mechanics. There are three major biomes, each with different plant and animal species. My role on the team was designing core mechanics and enemy AI systems. Thanks to all the hard work of our team, Evo won the best game award in our class!
